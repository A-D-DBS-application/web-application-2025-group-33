Platform Features

For Authors:

  • Paper Management: Upload PDF papers, update titles and subjects, and manage multiple versions
  • Collaboration: Invite other authors to collaborate on your papers and work together seamlessly
  • Publishing: Publish your papers to make them visible to companies and track download statistics
  • Insights: See which companies are interested in your work and view download counts
  • Reviews: Read and respond to reviews from companies and other authors

For Companies:

  • Discover Research: Browse all published papers with advanced search by title, author, or research field
  • AI Recommendations: Get personalized paper recommendations based on your research interests
  • Interest Management: Mark papers as interesting and build your personalized collection
  • Paper Access: Download PDFs of published papers and view detailed author information
  • Paper Insights: See download statistics, interest counts, and business relevance scores
  • Reviews: Leave reviews and ratings on papers to help other companies discover quality research
